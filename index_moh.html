  2. Acknowledge others.
  3. Think the best.
  4. Listen.
  5. Be inclusive.
  6. Speak kindly.
  7. Don't speak ill.
  8. Accept and give praise.
  9. Respect even a subtle "no".
  10. Respect others' opinions.
  11. Mind your body.
  12. Be agreeable.
  13. Keep it down.
